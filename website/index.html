The Summit Series

Summit Series Information

In September 1972, after years of preparation, hockey's Summit Series was played between the internationally dominant Soviet Union and the first collection of professionals to ever represent Canada - the very first "Team Canada".

Intended to improve relations between the two rivals, it ended up as the closest thing to open conflict ever experienced between the two nations.

"28,800 - The Power of Teamwork"

Named after the total number of seconds played over the eight-game series, "28,800 - The Power of Teamwork" is geared to inspire every Canadian with the lessons learned from the Summit Series, particularly those highlighting what can be acheived through teamwork.

The members of Team Canada 72 will use the legacy venture to benefit students, charities, and the nation's private community.
