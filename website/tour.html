72' Summit Series Tour

Thought you knew everything about the '72 Summit Series?

On September 2, 2017 - Eight members of Canada's fabled hockey team will return to Montreal to begin their '72 Summit Series Tour and share the stories and memories that have never been revealed - until now.

Forty-four years to the day, the legends of Team Canada 72 will relive the iconic eight-game series with their instructable and tenacious Soviet rivals.

Get to know the memories from the locker room and away from the rink - their hilarious stories about each other, and learn why these men became historic world-wide celebrities in their ownright. Unquestionably one of Canada's finest moments.

Take part in an unforgettable event, with state-of-the-art visual technology and sound design. Listen, as the players tell their previously untold stories - and how personal rivalries were resolved - a team formed in the crucible of competition.
