'72 Summit Series Tour

An evening with Canada's "Team of the Century"

In September 2017, members of Team Canada 72 will take part in a four-city tour of Canada, retracing their steps from the first half of the Summit Series.

Beginning in Montreal on the 2nd - exactly 44 years after Game One - the '72 Summit Series Tour will also appear in front of fans in Toronto, Winnipeg, and Vancouver.

Audiences will be immersed in the team's locker-room banter, as they share memories of those memorable 27 days in that September to remember!
