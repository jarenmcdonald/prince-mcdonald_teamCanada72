"Curse of Clara" Named Best Animated Programme

April 5th, 2017

"The Curse of Clara" has been named the Best Animated Programme at the 2017 Canadian Screen Awards.

As reported previously on this website, "Clara" stars the voice and image of Team Canada 72's own Phil Esposito.

Phil lent his his voice and Team Canada 72 character to the film, about a young ballet dancer taking on a role that's considered cursed; he plays her imaginary mentor, who helps her persevere.

The 30-minute special weaves elements of both "The Nutcracker" and the Summit Series into its storyline.

For more information about the film, visit www.curseofclara.com

Congratulations to Vickie Fagan and everyone else who made "Clara" such a success.
